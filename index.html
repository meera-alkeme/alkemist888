04Spiritual Engineer

Born in the East and raised in the West, David grew up enmeshed in two disparate cultures. At 30, a motorcycle journey through India became a three-year exploration of self, culture, and yoga — through which he discovered all the answers he sought lay within.

He lived in Auroville studying Sri Aurobindo's Integral Yoga and in 2016 he met Sadhguru, under whom he began studying Hatha Yoga — each path unlocking a deeper spiritual code.

With 10K+ meditation hours in Integral and Hatha yoga, David emerged as an undercover yogi. As a spiritual engineer, he looks at consciousness as a cipher to encrypt within both new and existing systems. His mission: elevate the state of human consciousness and build a conscious planet.
